Privacy Policy — Kakuro & Futoshiki

Last updated: June 20, 2026 · Publisher: POP GESTION · Contact: contact@pop-gestion.fr

This privacy policy explains how the mobile application Kakuro & Futoshiki (the "app") handles information. The app is published by POP GESTION ("we", "us").

Summary

Kakuro & Futoshiki is a puzzle game that works offline. The app does not create an account, does not request any personal data (no name, email or phone number) and does not send any personal data to our servers — we do not operate any server that collects your data.

Data stored on your device

To function, the app stores locally, on your device only:

  • your game progress and current puzzle;
  • your statistics (solved puzzles, times, streaks);
  • your preferences (language, theme, sound).

This data never leaves your device. It is deleted when you uninstall the app or clear its data from Android settings.

Third-party services

Advertising — Google AdMob

If the app displays ads, they are provided by Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, Google may collect and process certain technical information, including the device's advertising ID, IP address and interaction data. In the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom and Switzerland, a consent screen (UMP) is shown before any personalized advertising.
Google's policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
Google Ads settings: https://adssettings.google.com

Google Play Games (optional)

If you choose to sign in to Google Play Games (leaderboards, achievements, daily puzzle), authentication and related data are handled by Google under its own privacy policy. This sign-in is optional: the app remains fully playable without it.

Distribution — Google Play

The app is distributed through Google Play, which collects usage and diagnostic statistics under its own policy.

Children

The app is not specifically directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect their data. If ads are displayed, they are configured to comply with applicable family policies.

Your choices

  • You can reset or limit ad tracking in Android settings ("Privacy" > "Ads").
  • You can erase all local data by uninstalling the app or via "Settings > Apps > Kakuro & Futoshiki > Storage > Clear data".

Security

Because data stays on your device, its security depends on your device's security. We recommend protecting it with a passcode or biometrics.

Changes

We may update this policy. Any significant change will be indicated by updating the date at the top of this document.

Contact

For any question about this policy: contact@pop-gestion.fr
